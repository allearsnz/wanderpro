Returns Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Cancellations & Refunds

All sales are final. Cancellations made 48 hours or more before the experience date are eligible for a full refund to the original payment method. Cancellations within 48 hours of the experience date are non-refundable.

Gift Cards

Gift cards are non-refundable once delivered. Gift card codes are sent immediately upon order confirmation and cannot be cancelled or returned.

Weather & Operator Cancellations

If a tour is cancelled by the operator due to weather or unsafe conditions, you will be offered a full refund or the option to reschedule at no additional cost.

How to Request a Refund

Email info@wanderpro.us with your booking reference number and reason for cancellation. Our team will respond within 24 hours and process eligible refunds within 5–7 business days.

Modifications

Date or traveler changes more than 7 days before departure are free of charge subject to availability. Changes within 7 days may incur a fee from the tour operator.
